Welcome to The Abhiruci

At Abhiruchi, our endeavour is to provide in-depth training which augments your culinary skills. We are one of the best emerging culinary training institutions in Mumbai. Abhiruchi is the ultimate destination for you attain knowledge about how to excite taste buds.

Founder of the Abhiruchi

Why Us

Advice is like a food, and teaching is the recipe

Abhiruchi is a one-stop solution provider offering bespoke training as well as catering services. We offer a plethora of different cuisines that too at economical rates. We accept customised orders for special occasions and festivals. We are recognised and appreciated for our commitment to execute and deliver orders on schedule. While preparing orders, our paramount importance is on cleanliness and the qualities of products used as we resolutely believe that a dish is as good as the ingredients in it. We strive to not just achieve ours patron’s contentment, but to curate gastronomic cuisines which exceed their expectations.

We hold expertise in training people on how to proficiently satisfy an appetite with the help of various self-designed courses with certifications. Not only have we offered courses on baking but also on how to cook different cuisines. From cakes, breads, cookies and even popsicles and from ice-creams to sizzlers, Chinese, Mughlai and festival sweets, we conduct workshops on all of them. We teach people across proficiencies. We offer courses that are designed to fulfill specific needs and are taught in a structured format.

We assure you that we only use natural, raw and high quality of non-preserved ingredients and we do not indulge in any unethical practices like using cheap premixes, harmful chemicals or expired products.
